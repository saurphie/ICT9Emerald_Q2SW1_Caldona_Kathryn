Doberman Pinschers

Doberman Pinschers are very active dogs that require tons of exercise. Along with that, they can serve as guardians to your family. They are known for their upright ears although, they're naturally floppy. They're sweet and loyal to their owners but there's a tendency for them to develop a really close bond with only one person.

Pitbull Terrier

Bad boy on the outside but a soft cutie in the inside, introducing the Pitbull Terrier. These gentle and patient angels are bred to be courageous, especially during times of adversity. They tend to be stubborn at times which is why they require tons of training and socializing. Due to this, they may not be as friendly to other dogs. Keeping them on leash is the best way to prevent any other accidents from happening.

Siberian Husky

Huskies may look mean and dangerous because of their resemblance to wolves but truth is, they're just a bunch company-loving furballs. Leaving huskies alone when going out may not be a good idea for they can suffer from separation anxiety. They do love playing outdoors, especially in cold weather.
